# Welcome to Proxify

**Proxify is an AI-powered marketing platform that transforms how you create, distribute, and track your campaigns across multiple channels.**

Beyond simple link shortening, Proxify provides enterprise-level tools for marketers and content creators who need sophisticated campaign management, social media optimization, and detailed performance analytics.

## Key Capabilities

**Smart Link Management**
Create branded short links with AI optimization, link versioning, and change tracking. Perfect for campaigns that need professional, memorable URLs.

**Multi-Channel Distribution**
Distribute your content through multiple channels - each with its own destination URL for sophisticated campaign routing and testing.

**Professional QR Codes**
Generate branded QR codes with custom designs, download options, and integrated analytics tracking.

**Marketing Integrations**
Connect with leading marketing platforms like Parcelvoy, Mautic, and HubSpot to automate your campaign workflows.

**Enterprise Analytics**
Track performance across all channels with detailed UTM parameter management, click analytics, and campaign reporting.
