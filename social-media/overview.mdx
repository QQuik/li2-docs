## Access and Setup

### Accessing Social Media Optimization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to touchpoint advanced settings">
    1. Go to **Touchpoints** and select your touchpoint
    2. Navigate to the **Advanced** tab
    3. Find the **Meta Tags** section
    4. Toggle **Meta Tags** to **ON** to enable optimization

       ![Configure Meta Tag Elements Pn](/images/Configure-meta-tag-elements.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure optimization settings">
    **Premium Feature Access**:

    - Custom meta tags available for Premium tier users
    - Full customization control for images, titles, descriptions
    - Live preview functionality included
    - Professional social media presentation
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Default Behavior (Non-Premium)

**Automatic Fallback System**:

- Touchpoints without custom meta tags use default Li2.ai values
- Professional appearance maintained across all sharing
- Consistent branding with Li2.ai meta information
- Upgrade to Premium for full customization control

## Optimization Strategy

### Image Optimization

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Technical Requirements" icon="image" color="#10B981">
    **Format**: JPG or PNG
    **Size**: 1200 x 628 pixels
    **Quality**: High resolution for crisp display
  </Card>
  <Card title="Design Strategy" icon="palette" color="#10B981">
    **Branding**: Include logo and brand colors
    **Clarity**: Simple, clear design that works at small sizes
    **Context**: Visual should relate to destination content
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**Image Best Practices**:

- **Brand Consistency**: Use colors and fonts from your brand guidelines
- **High Contrast**: Ensure text and elements are clearly visible
- **Mobile Optimization**: Design looks good on small mobile previews
- **Relevant Content**: Image should relate to your destination page or offer

### Content Strategy

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Title optimization">
    **Effective Social Media Titles**:

    - **Length**: Keep under 60 characters for optimal display
    - **Action Words**: Use verbs that encourage engagement
    - **Benefits**: Focus on what users gain by clicking
    - **Clarity**: Make purpose immediately clear

    **Examples**:

    - "Join Our Free Marketing Masterclass"
    - "Download Your Complete SEO Guide"
    - "Get 30% Off Premium Features"
    - "Register for Industry Summit 2025"
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Description enhancement">
    **Compelling Social Descriptions**:

    - **Length**: 120-160 characters for optimal platform display
    - **Value Proposition**: Clearly state benefits or outcomes
    - **Keywords**: Include terms your audience searches for
    - **Call-to-Action**: End with clear next step

    **Description Framework**:

    - What: Briefly describe the offer or content
    - Why: Explain the benefit or value
    - How: Indicate the action needed
    - When: Add urgency if appropriate
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
