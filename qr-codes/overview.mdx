# QR Codes & Visual Tools Overview

Transform your marketing campaigns with professional, customizable QR codes that seamlessly integrate with your touchpoint strategy. Li2.ai's QR code tools go beyond basic generation to provide sophisticated design options, comprehensive analytics, and seamless integration with your broader marketing efforts.

## Professional QR Code Generation

Unlike basic QR code generators, Li2.ai's QR codes are fully integrated marketing assets that provide:

- **Advanced Customization** - Multiple design patterns, colors, and branding options
- **Professional Quality** - High-resolution output suitable for any marketing material
- **Integrated Analytics** - Complete scan tracking and performance measurement
- **Multi-Channel Strategy** - Independent routing from your digital links
- **Marketing Integration** - Seamless connection with your touchpoint campaigns

<Note>
**Marketing-First Approach**: Every QR code is automatically connected to your touchpoint analytics, UTM parameters, and campaign tracking - no separate setup required.
</Note>

## Getting Started with QR Codes

### Quick Start Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable QR in touchpoints">
    1. Create a new touchpoint or edit an existing one
    2. In the Integration Channel section, toggle **QR** to ON
    3. Your QR code is instantly generated with default settings
    4. Customize design using the **"Customize"** button
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Customize your design">
    1. Click **"Customize"** to open the design editor
    2. Choose your preferred **Body Pattern** from 6 options
    3. Select **Eye Pattern** for unique visual identity
    4. Pick colors that match your brand
    5. Upload your company logo for brand integration
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Download and deploy">
    1. Click **"Download"** to access format options
    2. Choose **PNG** (recommended 512x512) for most uses
    3. Select **SVG** for scalable professional applications
    4. Use in your marketing materials immediately
    5. Monitor performance through touchpoint analytics
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Best Practices for Implementation

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Design optimization">
    **Visual Design Tips**:
    - Choose high contrast colors for reliable scanning
    - Ensure logo doesn't interfere with QR functionality
    - Test scanning in various lighting conditions
    - Maintain consistent brand appearance across campaigns
    
    **Technical Considerations**:
    - Use recommended sizes for intended applications
    - Test QR codes before printing large quantities
    - Ensure adequate white space around QR codes
    - Consider scanning distance when determining size
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Marketing strategy">
    **Strategic Placement**:
    - Position QR codes where mobile scanning is convenient
    - Include clear call-to-action text near QR codes
    - Consider user context and motivation for scanning
    - Plan for mobile-optimized destination experiences
    
    **Performance Optimization**:
    - Use multi-channel routing for mobile-specific destinations
    - Set up UTM parameters for detailed tracking
    - Monitor scan rates and optimize placement accordingly
    - A/B testing different QR designs and placements
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
