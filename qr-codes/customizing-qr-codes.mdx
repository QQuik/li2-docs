# Customizing QR Codes

Make your QR codes stand out with custom patterns, colors, and your company logo. All changes update instantly so you can see exactly how your QR code will look.

## Accessing Customization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open QR customization">
    1. Go to **Touchpoints** and select a touchpoint with QR enabled
    2. Navigate to the **Details** tab
    3. In the **QR** section, click **"Customize"**
    4. The customization panel opens with live preview
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make your changes">
    All changes update instantly in the preview:

    - Choose different patterns and colors
    - Upload your company logo
    - See results immediately

      ![Customizing QR Codes Pn](/images/Customizing-QR-Codes.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Live Preview**: Changes appear instantly as you customize, so you can see exactly how your QR code will look before downloading.
</Note>

## Adding Your Logo

### Logo Requirements

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Supported Formats" icon="file" color="#7C3AED">
    **JPG, PNG, SVG**
    Maximum file size: 10MB
  </Card>
  <Card title="Best Practices" icon="lightbulb" color="#A855F7">
    **Square format, simple design**
    High resolution, transparent background (PNG)
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Upload Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prepare your logo">
    - Use a simple, clear design
    - Square or circular format works best
    - High resolution for quality reproduction
    - PNG with transparent background recommended
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload and preview">
    1. In the customization panel, find the **Logo** section
    2. Click **"Upload Logo"**
    3. Select your file (JPG, PNG, or SVG)
    4. Logo appears in the center of your QR code instantly
    5. Test scanning to ensure it still works properly
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Logo Placement**: Your logo will appear in the **center** of the QR code for maximum brand visibility.

## Design Management

### Testing Your Design

<Steps>
  <Step title="Preview your design">
    - All changes show instantly in the preview
    - Check that colors have good contrast
    - Ensure logo is clear and centered
    - Verify overall professional appearance
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test functionality">
    1. Download your customized QR code
    2. Test scanning with your smartphone
    3. Try different lighting conditions
    4. Verify it redirects correctly
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Reset to Default

<Warning>
  **Reset All Changes**: The "Reset to Default Style" button removes ALL customizations at once (patterns, colors, and logo). You cannot undo individual changes.
</Warning>

If you want to start over:

1. Click **"Reset to Default Style"** at the bottom of the customization panel
2. This removes all patterns, colors, and logos
3. QR code returns to standard black and white appearance
