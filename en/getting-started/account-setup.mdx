## Registration Methods

Li2.ai supports two convenient registration methods:

### Option 1: Google Sign-In (Recommended)

The fastest and most secure way to create your account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Li2.ai">
    Visit [li2.ai](https://li2.ai) and click **Sign Up Free** in the top right corner
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Google Sign-In">
    Click the **Continue with Google** button
  </Step>

  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Select your Google account and authorize Li2.ai to access your basic profile information (name and email address)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Setup">
    You'll be automatically signed in and redirected to your dashboard – ready to create your first touchpoint
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
**Why Google Sign-In?** It's more secure (no password to remember or manage), faster to set up, and you can revoke access anytime from your Google account settings.
</Tip>

### Option 2: Email + Verification Code

For users who prefer email-based registration:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Sign Up">
    Visit [li2.ai](https://li2.ai) and click **Sign Up Free**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Email">
    Provide your email address in the registration form
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive Verification Code">
    Check your email inbox for a verification code from Li2.ai. The email will arrive within 1-2 minutes.

    <Warning>
    **Check your spam folder** if you don't see the email. Add `noreply@li2.ai` to your contacts to ensure future emails are delivered.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Verification Code">
    Copy the 6-digit code from the email and paste it into the verification field on the registration page
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Password">
    Set a strong password for your account. Requirements:
    - Minimum 8 characters
    - At least one uppercase letter
    - At least one lowercase letter
    - At least one number
    - Special characters recommended but not required
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Registration">
    Click **Create Account** to finalize your registration. You'll be redirected to your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Account Security

Li2.ai takes security seriously to protect your touchpoints, analytics data, and campaigns:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Password Security">
    If you registered with email + password:
    - Use a unique password not shared with other services
    - Enable a password manager (1Password, LastPass, Bitwarden) for strong, unique passwords
    - Li2.ai encrypts passwords using industry-standard bcrypt hashing
    - Never share your password with team members – use organization invitations instead
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Email Verification">
    Your email address is verified during registration to ensure:
    - You own the email address and can receive important notifications
    - Password reset emails reach the correct recipient
    - Account security alerts are delivered promptly

    If you need to resend the verification email, click **Resend Code** on the registration page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="OAuth Security (Google Sign-In)">
    When using Google Sign-In:
    - Li2.ai only requests access to your basic profile (name and email)
    - No access to your Gmail, Google Drive, or other Google services
    - You can revoke Li2.ai's access anytime from your [Google Account Permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)
    - Your Google password is never shared with Li2.ai
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Session Management">
    - Active sessions expire after 30 days of inactivity
    - You can sign out manually from any device
    - Suspicious login attempts trigger email alerts
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Profile Configuration

After creating your account, complete your profile for better team collaboration and personalization:

### Access Your Profile

1. Click your **profile avatar** in the top right corner of the dashboard
2. Select **Profile Settings** from the dropdown menu

### Profile Information

Update these fields to personalize your account:

- **Display Name**: How your name appears to team members in shared organizations
- **Email Address**: Your primary contact email (verified during registration)
- **Avatar**: Upload a profile picture or use your Google profile photo
- **Language Preference**: Choose Vietnamese or English as your interface language
- **Timezone**: Set your timezone for accurate analytics time-series data

<Note>
**Language Switching**: Li2.ai supports both Vietnamese and English. You can switch languages anytime from your profile settings or the language selector in the navigation bar.
</Note>

### Notification Preferences

Configure which notifications you want to receive:

- **Weekly Analytics Reports**: Summary of your touchpoints' performance via email
- **Team Activity**: Notifications when team members create or modify touchpoints
- **System Updates**: Important platform updates and new feature announcements
- **Security Alerts**: Login from new devices, password changes, and suspicious activity

## What Happens After Registration?

Once you've created your account, here's what to expect:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Automatic Organization Creation">
    Li2.ai automatically creates a **personal organization** for you. This is your private workspace where you can create touchpoints, organize campaigns, and view analytics.

    Learn more about [organizations and team collaboration](/en/getting-started/organizations).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Dashboard Access">
    You'll be redirected to your dashboard at `app.li2.ai/org/`. This is your command center for:
    - Creating new touchpoints
    - Viewing analytics
    - Managing collections and tags
    - Accessing organization settings

    Explore the [dashboard overview](/en/getting-started/dashboard) to learn about key features.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ready to Create">
    Your account is now fully set up and you're ready to create your first touchpoint! Follow the [quick start guide](/en/getting-started/quick-start) for a step-by-step tutorial.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Account Registration During Touchpoint Creation

You can also create an account **during the touchpoint creation process**:

1. Visit [li2.ai](https://li2.ai) without logging in
2. Start creating a short link or QR code using the homepage tools
3. When you click **Generate**, you'll be prompted to create an account to save your touchpoint
4. Choose Google Sign-In or Email registration
5. After signing up, your touchpoint is automatically saved to your new account

This "create-first, register-later" flow is perfect for users who want to see the platform in action before committing to registration.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Verification email not received">
    **Solutions**:
    1. Check your spam/junk folder
    2. Verify you entered the correct email address
    3. Click **Resend Code** to request a new verification email
    4. Add `noreply@li2.ai` to your email contacts
    5. Try a different email address if the issue persists

    If none of these work, contact support at support@li2.ai
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Google Sign-In not working">
    **Common issues**:
    - **Pop-up blocked**: Allow pop-ups for li2.ai in your browser settings
    - **Third-party cookies disabled**: Enable cookies in browser settings
    - **Corporate firewall**: Contact your IT department to whitelist `li2.ai` and `accounts.google.com`

    Alternative: Use email + verification code registration instead
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Password doesn't meet requirements">
    Ensure your password has:
    - At least 8 characters
    - One uppercase letter (A-Z)
    - One lowercase letter (a-z)
    - One number (0-9)

    Example strong password: `Marketing2024!` (but use something unique to you)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Account already exists">
    If you see "An account with this email already exists":
    - Try signing in instead of signing up
    - Use the **Forgot Password** link if you don't remember your password
    - Check if you previously registered with Google Sign-In

    If you're certain you never created an account, contact support@li2.ai
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

<Tip>
**Use a Work Email**: If using Li2.ai for business, register with your company email address. This makes it easier to transfer ownership if you leave the company and helps with team organization.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Complete Your Profile**: Adding your name and profile picture helps team members identify you in shared organizations and improves collaboration.
</Tip>

<Warning>
**Don't Share Credentials**: Never share your Li2.ai login credentials with team members. Instead, create an organization and invite them as members with appropriate access levels.
</Warning>
