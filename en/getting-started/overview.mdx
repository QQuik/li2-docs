# Platform Overview

<Info>

## What is Li2.ai?

Li2.ai is a **modern platform for smart link management, customer conversion tracking, and affiliate program deployment**.

**"Smart links"** here are not just ordinary shortened URLs – they are unified **touchpoints** that can be expressed in multiple forms: short links (digital), QR codes (physical), NFC tags (physical). One touchpoint, multiple ways to reach customers.

**Li2.ai doesn't just count clicks** – we help you track the entire customer journey from **Visits → Leads → Sales**, with upcoming affiliate/partner management capabilities.

## Core Concept: Touchpoints

At the heart of Li2.ai is the concept of a **touchpoint** – a unified marketing asset that can be expressed as:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="QR Codes" icon="qrcode" color="#7C3AED">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Short Links" icon="link" color="#A855F7">

  </Card>
  <Card title="NFC Tags" icon="wifi" color="#5B21B6">
    Tap-to-connect experiences for retail, events, and smart packaging
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Each touchpoint is a **single source of truth** that can be distributed through multiple channels while maintaining unified analytics, destination control, and brand consistency.

## Key Capabilities

### Brand Customization

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Custom Domains">
    Use your own branded domain (e.g., `go.yourbrand.com`) instead of generic short URLs. This builds trust, reinforces brand identity, and improves click-through rates.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Custom URL Slugs">
    Create memorable, human-readable links like `yourbrand.com/summer-sale` instead of random character strings. Li2.ai also offers AI-powered slug generation for instant, contextual suggestions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Link Previews">
    Customize how your links appear when shared on social media platforms. Control the title, description, and preview image using Open Graph and Twitter Card meta tags.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Advanced Link Management

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Dynamic Destinations" icon="arrows-rotate">
    Change where a touchpoint redirects without reprinting QR codes or updating published links. Perfect for seasonal campaigns, A/B testing, and event promotions.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Collections & Tags" icon="folder-tree">
    Organize thousands of touchpoints into collections and apply tags for campaign-based filtering, team collaboration, and workflow management.
  </Card>

  <Card title="UTM Parameters" icon="chart-line">
    Built-in UTM builder for tracking campaign performance in Google Analytics and other marketing platforms. Automatically append tracking parameters to destination URLs.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Conversion Tracking" icon="bullseye">
    Set up conversion goals and track when users complete desired actions after clicking your touchpoints. Measure true ROI beyond simple click counts.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Real-Time Analytics

Li2.ai provides **comprehensive, real-time analytics** that go far beyond basic click counting:

- **Geographic Data**: See where your audience is located down to city-level precision
- **Device & Browser Intelligence**: Understand what devices and platforms your audience uses
- **Referrer Tracking**: Know exactly where traffic is coming from (social media, email, direct scans)
- **Time-Series Analysis**: Identify traffic patterns by hour, day, week, or month
- **Demographic Insights**: Profile your audience based on location and behavior
- **Campaign Performance**: Compare performance across campaigns, channels, and touchpoints

All analytics update in real-time, giving you instant visibility into campaign performance.

## How Li2.ai Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Touchpoint">
    Set your destination URL, customize the slug (or let AI generate one), and configure tracking parameters. Your touchpoint is instantly generated as a short link and QR code.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Distribute Across Channels">
    Download QR codes for print materials, copy short links for social media, or deploy NFC tags for tap-to-connect experiences. One touchpoint, infinite distribution possibilities.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Track Performance">
    Monitor real-time analytics as users interact with your touchpoints. See clicks, scans, geographic data, device types, and conversion events as they happen.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Optimize & Iterate">
    Use analytics insights to refine campaigns. Change destinations on the fly, test different messaging, and measure ROI across all channels.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Platform Advantages

<Note>
**Unified Management**: Unlike traditional tools that separate QR codes, link shorteners, and NFC management, Li2.ai treats them as expressions of a single touchpoint. This means unified analytics, simplified workflows, and consistent branding.
</Note>

<Tip>
**Dynamic Flexibility**: Touchpoints can be updated after distribution. Made a typo in your destination URL? Running a limited-time promotion? Simply update the touchpoint's destination – no need to reprint materials or update published links.
</Tip>
