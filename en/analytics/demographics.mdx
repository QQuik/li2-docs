## Actionable Insights

### Mobile-First Optimization
If 70%+ traffic is mobile:
- Optimize destination pages for mobile
- Use mobile-friendly QR code sizes
- Test mobile payment flows
- Prioritize mobile app development

### Regional Expansion
High performance in unexpected countries:
- Launch region-specific campaigns
- Translate content to local languages
- Partner with local influencers
- Establish local presence

### Channel Reallocation
Low-performing channels:
- Reduce spend on underperforming platforms
- Double down on high-ROI channels
- Test new platforms for untapped audience
- Optimize underperforming creative

## Best Practices

<Tip>
**Segment Before Optimizing**: Don't optimize for "all users" - segment by device, location, source and optimize each segment individually.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Track Anomalies**: Sudden spikes in unexpected countries or devices often indicate bot traffic or viral sharing. Investigate before celebrating.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Cross-Reference Data**: Combine demographic data with conversion rates. High traffic + low conversion indicates targeting mismatch.
</Tip>
