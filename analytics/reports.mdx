**Key Performance Indicators**:
- **Total Visits**: Complete visitor count with percentage change vs last week
- **Unique Visitors**: Individual user engagement with growth tracking
- **Countries Reached**: Geographic distribution and international engagement
- **Active Touchpoints**: Number of touchpoints generating traffic
