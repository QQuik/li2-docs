## Step 1: Complete Your Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access your profile settings">
    From your dashboard, click on your profile icon in the top right corner and select **"Settings"**.

    You'll see the Settings page with your profile information on the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in your basic information">
    In the **"Edit Profile Information"** section, complete:

    **Name**: Your full name or business name for professional touchpoints

    **Email**: Verify this is correct - you'll receive important notifications here

    **Address** (Optional): Useful for geographic analytics and compliance

    **Date of Birth** (Optional): For account verification if needed

    Click **"Save"** when complete.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email address">
    If you haven't already, check your email for a verification message from Li2.ai.

    **Why this matters**: Verified accounts get full access to analytics and can set up integrations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Step 2: Secure Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review your password">
    In the **"Update Password"** section, ensure you have a strong password:

    - At least 12 characters long
    - Mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
    - Not used on other accounts

    Update if needed and click **"Save"**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable two-factor authentication (Recommended)">
    Scroll down to **"Multi-factor Authentication"** section.

    Set up **"Authenticator App"** for enhanced security:

    - Use Google Authenticator, Authy, or similar apps
    - Follow the setup instructions provided
    - This protects your marketing data and analytics

    <Tip>
      Two-factor authentication is especially important to protect your marketing data and analytics.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 4: Prepare for Success

<Steps>
  <Step title="Plan your first campaign">
    Before creating touchpoints, consider:

    **Campaign Goal**: What do you want to track? (website visits, downloads, sign-ups)

    **Distribution Channels**: Will you use links, QR codes, or both?

    **Organization**: Think about tag names for campaign organization

    **Destination**: Have your target URL ready
  </Step>
  <Step title="Gather your assets (if needed)">
    For advanced features, prepare:

    **Custom Images**: For social media optimization (optional)

    **UTM Strategy**: Campaign names and tracking parameters (optional)

    **Brand Assets**: Logo for QR code customization (optional)

    <Note>
      Don't worry - you can add these later! The basic touchpoint creation only requires a destination URL.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>
