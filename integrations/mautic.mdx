## Planned Capabilities

### Lead Management
- **Automatic Contact Creation**: New contacts from touchpoint interactions
- **Progressive Profiling**: Enhanced contact data from touchpoint analytics
- **Behavioral Scoring**: Lead scoring based on touchpoint engagement patterns
- **Segmentation**: Advanced audience segmentation using touchpoint data

### Marketing Automation
- **Email Campaigns**: Triggered email sequences from touchpoint interactions
- **Multi-Channel Messaging**: Coordinate email, SMS, and social media campaigns
- **Dynamic Content**: Personalized content based on touchpoint behavior
- **A/B Testing**: Optimize campaigns with integrated testing capabilities

### Analytics & Attribution
- **Campaign Performance**: Track touchpoint contribution to marketing goals
- **Conversion Tracking**: Full funnel attribution from touchpoint to conversion
- **ROI Analysis**: Measure marketing automation return on investment
- **Custom Reports**: Detailed reporting on integrated campaign performance
